## Executive Summary

We audited 10 competitors -- 5 identified by Shawn Durrant and 5 discovered through SERP analysis. The finding is striking: **not a single competitor in the Ogden/Wasatch Front excavation market has a competent digital presence.** The best competitor site (Next Construction) has ~10 thin pages. Most have 5-6 pages or no website at all. Zero competitors have a blog, location pages, or schema markup. The highest review count among SERP competitors is 19 (RGJ Construction). AccuRite already has 49 Google reviews and ranks #1 for "excavation contractor Ogden Utah" -- a well-executed redesign will create an insurmountable lead.

# Strategic Analysis

---

## Strategic Opportunities for AccuRite

### 1. SEO Dominance is Wide Open
Across all 10 competitors, the most pages any site has is ~10 (Next Construction). Most have 5-6 or none at all. Zero competitors have location pages, blogs, or schema markup. AccuRite's planned 18 location pages + 10+ service pages + blog will surpass every competitor combined by 5-10x.

### 2. Content Marketing = Uncontested Territory
Zero of 10 competitors have a blog. Not one. Even basic content (5-10 articles on excavation topics, FAQs, project case studies) will give AccuRite 100% of the content marketing footprint in this market.

### 3. Reviews Are AccuRite's Existing Moat
AccuRite already has **49 Google reviews** -- more than all 10 competitors combined. The next closest is C.E. Butters with ~74 across all platforms (but likely far fewer on Google specifically), then RGJ with 19 at 5.0 stars. The new site should amplify this advantage with prominent review displays and AggregateRating schema.

### 4. Service Page Depth Wins
The best competitor site lumps services onto 1-2 pages. RGJ lists 24 services on a single homepage. Creating deep, keyword-optimized individual service pages will dominate every service-specific search.

### 5. Location Pages = Uncontested Local Search
Only Parker Rock 'n' Dirt has any location pages (2). Triple H claims 25+ cities but has zero location pages. Rock Solid claims 6 counties with zero pages. AccuRite's planned 18 city pages will capture local search traffic no competitor is even attempting.

### 6. Schema Markup = Rich Results Monopoly
Zero of 10 competitors use structured data. Implementing LocalBusiness, Service, AggregateRating, FAQ, and BreadcrumbList schema will give AccuRite rich search results that no competitor can match.

### 7. Google Business Profile Optimization
Most competitors have weak or nonexistent GBP profiles. Optimizing AccuRite's GBP with photos, posts, service descriptions, and review responses will compound the website SEO gains.

### 8. Military/Government Credibility
AccuRite has real military project experience (Camp Williams, Hill AFB, sniper tower, National Park Service praise). Skinner has government project capability (woman-owned helps with contracts). Dedicated case study pages with photos will differentiate AccuRite.

### 9. Active Licensing as Trust Signal
Multiple competitors show inactive licenses on BuildZoom (Next Construction expired Nov 2023, Triple H inactive, Ormond inactive). AccuRite should prominently display active E100 license and general engineering credentials on every page.

### Competitive Threat Level

| Threat | Level | Why |
|--------|-------|-----|
| Digital/SEO | **VERY LOW** | No competitor has meaningful SEO. The field is wide open. |
| Offline/Reputation | **MODERATE** | Established players (Ormond 56yr, Butters 50yr, Skinner 39yr) have deep referral networks |
| Bid Competition | **MODERATE** | Government and commercial bids are won on price/relationships, not websites |
| Review Velocity | **LOW** | AccuRite's 49 reviews already dominates; only RGJ has quality reviews (19 at 5.0) |

**Bottom line:** A well-executed site with 50+ optimized pages, location targeting, content marketing, and schema markup will dominate online lead generation in this market. No competitor is even close to competing digitally.
