# Keyword Research: 10 Service Pages

**Prepared for:** AccuRite Excavation
**Date:** March 12, 2026
**Market:** Ogden, Utah / Wasatch Front / Northern Utah

---

## Executive Summary

This report covers keyword research for AccuRite Excavation's 10 core service pages. Each service page targets a primary keyword cluster with local intent modifiers (Ogden, Utah, Wasatch Front, near me). Volume estimates are based on national benchmarks from industry SEO sources with local scaling applied (Utah metro = ~1-3% of national volume).

**Key findings:**
- "Excavation" umbrella terms have the highest volume nationally (158,000+ monthly US searches across all excavation keywords)
- Rock walls / retaining walls have surprisingly strong search demand, especially with cost-related queries
- Septic system keywords carry very high commercial intent and low competition in the Ogden market
- Demolition keywords are competitive nationally but wide open locally in Weber County
- Water features / ponds is a niche with low volume but very high conversion intent and low competition
- Government projects page should target "public works" and "municipal" keyword clusters rather than "government excavation"
- Long-tail FAQ queries are the fastest path to ranking for a new site

**Competitor keyword patterns observed:**
- Rock Solid Excavation: "excavation & demolition services," "retaining wall contractors," "building demolition"
- Triple H Excavating: "excavating & utility work," "rock products," "hauling"
- Skinner Excavating: "Utah land development," "subdivision development," "public works projects"
- CE Butters Companies: "excavation contractors Northern Utah," "septic system contractor," "demolition and wreckage removal"
- RGJ Construction: "excavation and land clearing near Ogden," "site preparation," "sewer excavation"
- NEXT Construction: "excavations," "hauling equipment," "aggregate services"
