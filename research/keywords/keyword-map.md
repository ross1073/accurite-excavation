# AccuRite Excavation - Keyword Map

**Date:** 2026-03-12

This document indexes the keyword research for the AccuRite redesign. Detailed keyword data lives in the research files below.

## Service Page Keywords
See: [keyword-research-service-pages.md](keyword-research-service-pages.md)

10 service pages researched with primary, secondary, and long-tail keywords.

### Priority Order (by volume + intent + competition)
1. Rock Walls & Retaining Walls - highest local volume, geographic advantage
2. Demolition - good volume, clear cost queries
3. Residential Excavation - core service, basement excavation queries
4. Septic Systems - low competition, high project values, rural market
5. Grading & Land Clearing - good volume, pairs with other services
6. Underground Utilities - steady demand, less competitive
7. Commercial Projects - credibility play, lower search volume
8. Hauling & Delivery - high "near me" volume, supports other pages
9. Water Features & Ponds - niche, low competition, high margins
10. Government Projects - credibility page, minimal organic volume

### Recommended Title Format
`[Service] in Ogden & Northern Utah | AccuRite Excavation`
