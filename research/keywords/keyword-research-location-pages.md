# Keyword Research: 18 Location/Service Area Pages

**Prepared for:** AccuRite Excavation
**Date:** March 12, 2026
**Market:** Ogden, Utah / Wasatch Front / Northern Utah

---

## Executive Summary

This report covers keyword research for 18 city-specific service area pages across Weber County, Davis County, and Box Elder County. The cities range from Ogden (pop. ~90,000) to Huntsville (pop. ~600). The data combines national keyword volume benchmarks for excavation-related terms with local competitive analysis and geographic intent signals.

**Key findings:**
- "Excavation contractor [city] Utah" is the primary keyword pattern, with meaningful search volume only for the top 5-6 cities
- Smaller cities (pop. <10,000) have near-zero city-specific search volume but serve as geographic authority signals and capture long-tail "near me" queries
- Rock wall / retaining wall keywords are a strong differentiator in this market, especially along the Wasatch bench
- Septic system keywords are relevant for unincorporated/rural areas (Eden, Huntsville, Willard, Perry)
- "Near me" queries dominate the excavation space nationally and are served by Google Business Profile proximity, not individual pages
