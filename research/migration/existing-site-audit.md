## Summary

The existing site has 113 total URLs in its sitemap (11 pages, 27 posts, 5 categories, 5 tags, 65 attachment pages). ## Critical SEO Issues

### 1. Title Tag Crisis
Only ~5 distinct title tags exist across 38 pages+posts:
- **"Quality Utah Exacavation"** - used on 10 of 11 pages (note: "Excavation" is misspelled)
- **"Utah Excavation"** - used on most posts
- **"Utah Ogden Excavation"** - used on some posts
- **"Utah Residential Excavation Contractor"** - only 1 post
- **"Davis County Waterline Construction"** - only 1 post
- **"Commercial Construction"** - only 1 post

**Impact:** Google cannot differentiate pages. Every page competes with itself for the same generic terms.

### 2. Meta Description Duplication
Only 4 of 38 pages+posts have unique meta descriptions. Two defaults are reused everywhere:

**Pages default:** "Excavation, rock walls, water features, ponds all done with reliable Utah company, We to top soil, sand, utilities, back fill and everything you need. We love to dig in the dirt."

**Posts default:** "Top quality Utah Excavation company servicing the Wasatch Front, Idaho, Colorado, Wyoming, and the western states."

### 3. Typos in SEO Metadata
- "Exacavation" in site-wide title tag (all pages)
- "commerical" and "stront" in homepage meta description
- "contrator" in residential excavation contractor meta

### 4. Duplicate/Confusing Page Structure
- `/our-services/services`, `/our-services/services-2`, `/our-services/services-3` (three child pages under services)
- `/the-accurite-excavation-promise/about-us-2` and `/about-us-2-2` (duplicate about pages)
- `/get-a-quote` (post) vs `/request-a-quote` (page) -- two quote request URLs

### 5. 65 Indexed Attachment Pages
WordPress attachment pages are indexed in the sitemap. These provide zero SEO value and dilute crawl budget. Examples:
- `/accurite-gallery/20191028_141725`
- `/steam-plant-hill-air-force-base-utah/179cc4fe-ed5b-43b4-9c17-9f73b45e7170`

All 65 must be redirected to parent pages or return 410 Gone.

### 6. Posts Used as Pages
Some posts function as pages: `/get-a-quote`, `/our-work-past-projects`. Content type distinction was not maintained.

### 7. No Trailing Slashes
All URLs served without trailing slashes. New site should match this or redirect.
