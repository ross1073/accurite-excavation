## Excavation Services in Harrisville, UT

Harrisville sits in a useful middle position in Weber County, close to Ogden's services and north toward North Ogden, with the Wasatch foothills rising to the east. It's mostly residential, and the character of the work here reflects that: utility replacements, foundation drainage repairs, grading projects, and retaining walls on properties where the bench terrain creates grade challenges.

We cover Harrisville as part of our core Weber County service territory.

### Retaining Walls on Harrisville Bench Properties

The east side of Harrisville transitions toward the mountain bench, and some of those properties have grade changes that need proper treatment. Unretained slopes erode, shift, and become safety concerns over time, especially in Utah's freeze-thaw cycles.

We excavate for [retaining wall](/services/rock-walls-retaining-walls) footings, manage the grading behind the wall, and handle drainage installation so water doesn't build up and push against the structure. We work with boulders, concrete block, and timber depending on the project requirements and your preferences.

### Foundation Drainage and Yard Grading

Clay soils hold water, and flat or gently sloping lots in Harrisville can accumulate moisture near foundations if the original grading has settled or shifted. This is a common situation in neighborhoods that were built decades ago.

We reestablish proper slope away from the foundation, extend or redirect downspout discharge, and install French drains where surface grading alone isn't enough. [Drainage grading](/services/grading-drainage) done correctly is one of the better investments a homeowner can make in protecting their foundation.

### Utility Trenching

Harrisville homeowners call us for utility work when water or sewer lines need replacement, or for new utility installations related to additions or detached structures. We trench, coordinate with your licensed plumber, backfill with proper compaction, and restore the surface.

[Utility trenching](/services/utility-trenching) in clay soils requires attention to backfill compaction. Poorly compacted clay settles significantly over time, leaving depressions in driveways and yards. We take compaction seriously on every trench we backfill.

### Residential Excavation

For homeowners in Harrisville building additions, finishing basements, or starting new construction on vacant lots, we handle [excavation](/services/excavation) to depth, shoring where needed for tight cuts, and coordination with your foundation contractor.

Bench-area properties may encounter harder material than typical valley floor soil. We assess conditions during the estimate process and factor difficulty into our pricing honestly.

### Demolition

We handle small residential demolition in Harrisville, including detached garages, sheds, old concrete slabs, and driveways. Debris is hauled off and the site is left graded. Permits are pulled when required by the city.
