## Excavation Services in West Haven

West Haven has gone from a quiet agricultural community to one of the most active construction markets in Weber County. The city is adding residents, homes, and infrastructure at a pace that keeps excavation contractors busy. Accurite Excavation is positioned to serve West Haven's growth with site prep, grading, and utility work scaled to meet new construction demand.

The flat terrain here is a different kind of work from the hillside projects up in North Ogden or Pleasant View. It's not simpler, just different. Clay soils, high water tables in some areas, and the scale of subdivision development all require their own expertise.

### New Construction Site Preparation

Every home built in West Haven starts with properly prepared ground. Site prep involves clearing vegetation or old agricultural material, rough grading to establish the building pad at the correct elevation, fine grading to meet builder specifications, and compaction to achieve the soil density required by structural engineers.

West Haven's clay soils need particular attention during compaction. Clay expands when wet and shrinks when dry, and structures built on improperly compacted clay will move over time. We use proper equipment and testing procedures to get compaction right.

We work with builders to match their schedule and hand off a pad that meets their requirements. When we're done, your foundation crew walks onto a site that's ready to go.

### Subdivision and Development Grading

Large-scale grading for new subdivisions is a significant part of what we do in West Haven. Mass grading moves and redistributes large volumes of soil to establish final grades across an entire development before individual lots are finished.

Proper mass grading accounts for stormwater management, ensures lots drain to the street or designated drainage areas, and sets up the correct finished floor elevations relative to roadways and utility connections.

We handle grading plans from civil engineers and coordinate with project superintendents to keep multi-phase developments on schedule.

### Utility Trenching and Installation

New subdivisions need complete utility infrastructure. Water mains, sewer lines, storm drains, and dry utilities all require trenching, pipe bedding, installation, backfill, and compaction before roads and lots can be finished.

We trench to specified depths and grades, prepare proper pipe bedding material, and backfill and compact in lifts to prevent settlement. Utility trenches that settle create problems for roads and landscaping above them; doing the compaction right the first time prevents callbacks.

Individual lot utility connections, including water service and sewer lateral from the main to the home, are part of our residential work as well.

### Drainage and Dewatering

West Haven's lower-lying areas have water table conditions that require attention. Foundation excavation in spring can hit groundwater before reaching the required depth. We have dewatering equipment to manage this so excavation and foundation work can proceed on schedule.

Surface drainage is another consideration on flat terrain. Without proper grading, water from rain and irrigation has nowhere to go. We establish drainage grades that direct water to the street or detention areas and away from home foundations.

### Road Subgrade Preparation

Subdivision roads need a properly prepared subgrade before base gravel and asphalt go down. We grade and compact subgrade to specifications, which determines how well the road surface holds up over years of traffic.

Subgrade work in clay soil sometimes requires over-excavation and replacement with imported granular material if the native soil is too expansive to support pavement. We work with civil engineers to determine what each road section requires.

### Working With Builders in West Haven

We understand that new construction runs on tight schedules. Delays in site prep push back every trade that follows. We show up when we say we will, finish on time, and communicate if conditions change the scope.

We've built relationships with builders and developers in the Ogden area and take those working relationships seriously. Builders who call us back for the next project are the measure of whether we did good work on the last one.

### Retaining Walls and Grade Management

While West Haven is mostly flat, properties along the edges of developments sometimes need [retaining walls](/services/rock-walls-retaining-walls) to manage grade transitions between lots, roads, and drainage features. We handle footing excavation, wall construction, and drainage installation behind the wall. Block walls and boulder walls are both options depending on height and aesthetic.

### Demolition and Site Clearing

Before new construction can begin on some West Haven lots, existing structures need to come down. We handle [demolition](/services/demolition) of old agricultural buildings, concrete slabs, and outbuildings. All debris is loaded and [hauled off](/services/hauling-delivery) so the site is clean and ready for grading.

### Septic Systems on West Haven Properties

Some West Haven properties, particularly those on the western edge of the development area, require [septic systems](/services/septic-systems) rather than municipal sewer connections. We dig tank pits and drain field trenches to designer specifications and coordinate with Weber County Health Department on permitting and inspection.
