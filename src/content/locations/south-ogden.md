## Excavation Services in South Ogden

South Ogden is a city that's been established for decades in some areas and still growing in others. That range of development means the excavation work here is varied. Some customers need us because they're updating old infrastructure that's finally failed. Others are adding to their properties or building on undeveloped east bench lots. We handle both ends of that spectrum.

Accurite Excavation is based in the Ogden area and works regularly in South Ogden. We know the terrain shifts as you move east, and we adjust our approach depending on what a given lot looks like.

### Site Preparation and Grading

Site prep in South Ogden covers new construction pads, garage additions, detached shop buildings, and accessory dwelling units. Getting a flat, properly compacted surface is the foundation for everything built on top of it.

Valley floor lots are generally easier to work with, but the clay soil requires attention during compaction. East bench lots involve more complex grading, especially where slope requires cut-and-fill work to create usable flat areas.

We handle rough grading and finish grading, and we make sure drainage slopes away from structures. Water management on a sloped lot is something that gets ignored until it's a problem; we address it at the grading stage.

### Utility Trenching and Replacement

Older South Ogden neighborhoods have aging utility infrastructure. Sewer laterals, water service lines, and drainage systems installed 40-60 years ago are hitting the end of their service life. We dig the trenches, remove failed pipe, and install replacements to current code.

Utility work in heavy clay soil requires care during backfill and compaction. Poorly compacted backfill settles over time and pulls down yard surfaces, driveways, and anything else above the trench. We compact in lifts to get it right.

New utility installations for additions and accessory structures follow the same process, connecting new service to existing points at the property line or main structure.

### Retaining Walls

[Retaining walls](/services/rock-walls-retaining-walls) come up regularly on east bench South Ogden properties. Sloped lots need walls to create usable yard space, hold back upslope material, and protect structures from slope movement.

We build boulder walls, segmental block walls, and concrete walls depending on what the application calls for. Boulder walls are a natural fit for rocky bench terrain where the material blends with the surroundings. Block walls work better in residential settings where a cleaner line is preferred.

Any wall holding back significant soil needs drainage behind it. We install gravel backfill and perforated drain pipe to relieve hydrostatic pressure, which is what prevents walls from leaning or failing over time.

### Demolition and Clearing

Infill development in South Ogden occasionally requires demolition of old structures before new construction begins. We handle concrete demolition, slab removal, and site clearing to prepare lots for new builds.

Old concrete flatwork, whether that's a driveway, patio, or outbuilding slab, needs to be broken up and hauled out before grading can begin. We do that work efficiently and dispose of material properly.

### Drainage Corrections

South Ogden's clay soils hold water. Properties with drainage problems see water pooling against foundations, saturated yards, and basement moisture issues. Correcting drainage means redirecting where water goes before it reaches problem areas.

We install French drains, swales, and catch basins to intercept surface and subsurface water and channel it away from structures. Proper grading to establish positive drainage away from the foundation is part of most grading projects we do.
